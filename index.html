Learn the Basics

With this documentation, you can easily grasp the basics of BO3's Mod Tools, going from knowing nothing about game development to making your own custom maps and releasing them onto the workshop, filling any gaps in your knowledge along the way.

Search through a collection of common errors, their explanations & respective fixes to aid you when debugging your map. Explore all our in-depth guides, covering everything about the Launcher, Radiant, the Asset Editor (APE), scripting in GSC and more.
